CALL Trade (Up ↑)

ඔබ හිතනවා price ඉහළ යනවා කියලා. Expiry time ට price ඔබේ entry price ට වඩා ඉහළ නම් — WIN!

Entry: $1.0850 → Expiry: $1.0873
✅ WIN — Price went UP

PUT Trade (Down ↓)

ඔබ හිතනවා price පහළ යනවා කියලා. Expiry time ට price ඔබේ entry price ට වඩා පහළ නම් — WIN!

Entry: $1.0850 → Expiry: $1.0821
✅ WIN — Price went DOWN

🟢 Green (Bullish) Candle

Open < Close. Price ඉහළ ගිහින් close වෙලා. CALL signal.

🔴 Red (Bearish) Candle

Open > Close. Price පහළ ගිහින් close වෙලා. PUT signal.

📏 Wick (Shadow)

High/Low range. Long wicks = price rejected that level = reversal signal.

Uptrend

Higher highs + Higher lows. Trade CALL ලා on pullbacks. Trend is your friend!

L1 → H1 → L2 → H2 →
(L2 > L1, H2 > H1) ✅

Downtrend

Lower highs + Lower lows. Trade PUT ලා on bounces. Follow the trend down.

H1 → L1 → H2 → L2 →
(H2 < H1, L2 < L1) ✅

Sideways / Range

Price moves between Support and Resistance. Trade bounces off levels.

Resistance: ━━━━━━━━
Support: ━━━━━━━━

🎯 Support & Resistance

Support (Floor)

Price repeatedly bounces UP from this level. Strong support zone = good CALL entry point. Breaks below = trend change!

Resistance (Ceiling)

Price repeatedly bounces DOWN from this level. Strong resistance = good PUT entry point. Breaks above = bullish breakout!

Price Chart (simplified)

Resistance: $1.0900
↕ Price bouncing
Support: $1.0850

✅ Near Support → Trade CALL

✅ Near Resistance → Trade PUT

⚠️ Mid-range → Skip trade

Chapter 4

Best Indicators for Pocket Option

Platform built-in tools use කරලා signals confirm කරන්න

RSI

RSI – Relative Strength Index

⭐ Most Used

0–100 scale. Market overbought/oversold conditions හදුනා ගනී. Pocket Option ට default setting: 14 period.

70+ = Overbought

→ Trade PUT

30–70 = Neutral

→ Wait

30 = Oversold

→ Trade CALL

MACD

MACD – Trend & Momentum

Two moving averages cross-over use කරලා trend direction change detect කරනවා. Settings: 12, 26, 9.

🟢 Bullish Signal

MACD line crosses ABOVE signal line → CALL

🔴 Bearish Signal

MACD line crosses BELOW signal line → PUT

BB

Bollinger Bands – Volatility

3 bands: Middle (SMA), Upper band, Lower band. Price bands ල outside ගිහින් return වෙනවා typically.

Touch Lower Band → CALL

Price too low, bounce expected

Touch Upper Band → PUT

Price too high, pullback expected

MA

Moving Average – Trend Direction

Price average over time. 50 MA + 200 MA use කරලා trend confirm කරන්න. Simple but powerful.

Price above 50 MA → CALL

Uptrend confirmed

Price below 50 MA → PUT

Downtrend confirmed

Proven Trading Strategies

Beginners ලා prove කරගෙන ඉන්න 4 strategies

1

RSI + Support/Resistance Strategy

⭐ Best for Beginners — 60-65% Win Rate

CALL Signal:

✅ Price near Support level

✅ RSI below 30 (oversold)

✅ Bullish candle forming

✅ Enter 1–5 min expiry

PUT Signal:

✅ Price near Resistance level

✅ RSI above 70 (overbought)

✅ Bearish candle forming

✅ Enter 1–5 min expiry

💡 Pro tip: RSI divergence (price makes new high but RSI doesn't) = very strong reversal signal!
2

Trend Following (MA Crossover)

📊 Intermediate — 58-63% Win Rate

CALL Setup:

✅ 9 EMA crosses above 21 EMA

✅ Price above both MAs

✅ MACD histogram positive

✅ 5 min expiry

PUT Setup:

✅ 9 EMA crosses below 21 EMA

✅ Price below both MAs

✅ MACD histogram negative

✅ 5 min expiry

3

Bollinger Band Bounce Strategy

⚡ Fast Scalping — Range markets

Sideways market ල trade කරන best strategy. Price BB lower band touch කළාම CALL, upper band touch කළාම PUT.

Timeframe

1M / 5M

Expiry

1–3 min

BB Settings

20, 2.0

4

Pin Bar Reversal Strategy

🎯 Advanced — High accuracy at key levels

Candlestick pattern use කරලා reversals catch කරනවා. Pin bar = long wick, small body.

✅ Long wick = rejection of price level

✅ Small body = indecision

✅ Must form at Support/Resistance

✅ Wait for next candle confirm

→ CALL

→ PUT

Chapter 8

Trading Glossary

Common trading terms සිංහලෙන්

Payout

WIN කළ විට ලැබෙන profit percentage. 80% payout = $10 bet WIN → +$8 profit.

Expiry Time

Trade close වෙන time. 1 min expiry = 1 minute පසු result.

Strike Price

Trade open කළ price (entry price). WIN/LOSS ගණනය කිරීමේ base.

OTC Assets

Over-the-Counter. Real market close ව ද trade කරන්නට virtual assets. Weekends available.

Win Rate

Total trades ලෙස WIN කළ percentage. 60% = 100 trades ලෙස 60 wins. Break-even: 55.6% at 80% payout.

Drawdown

Peak balance සිට current balance දක්වා decline. 20%+ drawdown = serious warning sign.

Kelly Criterion

Mathematically optimal bet size calculate කිරීමේ formula. Win rate + payout use කරනවා.

Martingale

Loss ගත් trade ලෙස next trade double කරීම. High risk — avoid unless you understand fully!

Pip

Price movement ලෙස smallest unit. EUR/USD: 1 pip = 0.0001.

Volatility

Price movement ලෙස size. High volatility = large swings = higher risk AND opportunity.
